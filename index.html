Intro

After I finished watching the hit HBO series Gentleman Jack, I couldn’t help myself to want to know more about Anne Lister deeply and thoroughly. During the time, I was reading the famous Wuthering Heights written by Emily Brontë, the writer who lived near Anne at the time.
